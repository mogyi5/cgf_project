Stephen Hawking

Earlier today, popular physicist, cosmologist and professor at Cambridge University has passed away aged 76, on pi day. He popularised black holes and deep questions about the universe, and he will be dearly missed.

Jupiter

Recently, we have learned more about Jupiter - that its clouds penetrate more than 3000km below the surface, but the atmosphere only makes up 1% of the planet's mass. Below the atmospere though, Jupiter is completely fluid.

72-qubit Google Computer

Google has finally unveiled their new 72 quantum-bit computer, which will be faster than any other existing computer. Unlike traditional bits, quantum bits can be 0, 1 or a mixture of the two - making things more complex. Good luck!
